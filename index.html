About

I have a rubik's complex.
That is, once I see the puzzle, I can't put it down until it's solved...

In my personal life, being an avid competitive gamer scratched this itch (PC gaming anyone?). That, and the feeling of crushing a guitar lick, or the warm-fuzzy of fixing bugged code.

But professionally, I needed change. Though I found success in my beginnings on BA & PMO teams, the macro-oriented responsibilities under-utilized my technical skills and passion for detail. I took command, made a career shift to data science, and found what was missing.
Because afterall, data science = puzzles galore.

I took my first plunge into data analytic work at a Fortune 500, solving customer pain points in a vast sea of bigdata consumer features. More recently, I've been leveling up my technical skills in advanced SQL querying, and Python machine learning techniques through a combination of scholastic work (udemy bootcamps) and pet projects, which my portfolio below details.

I will continue to learn, evolving my strategies in order to solve the different data analytic puzzles on my journey. All the while sticking to my master plan for winning the puzzles:
Let data tell the story.

Data Wrangling, Cleaning, Exploration

I work data science efforts on a project basis, and after taking the first step of aligning on a business problem to solve, I begin the data cleaning and exploration steps that are key to garnering successful insights, predictions, and overall - projects.

Here's how:

My Data Skills & Tools

  • Querying Softwares:
    SQL, NOSQL, PostgreSQL
  • Data Navigation fundamentals:
    SELECT, FROM, WHERE, ORDER BY, TOP
  • Aggregate functions and viewing:
    SUM, COUNT, GROUP BY, HAVING
  • Window functions to pass data:
    PARTITION BY
  • Joining datasets:
    Proper primary-foreign key relationships, INNER JOIN, (and sometimes) OUTER/LEFT/RIGHT JOIN

Client Interfacing, Visualizations

I make sure to view projects end to end, ensuring inclusion of all key external & internal stakeholders along the way.
After establishing a business problem, getting clean data together and having mined insights from proper data exploration, I work towards reporting key findings in a clean, understandable way. This is often through Tableau visualizations, and PowerPoint presentation on video or in-person conferences. I enjoy the details, but I understand the importance of translating dense analytics into actionable, human-readable take-aways.

I do this by:

  • Creating tools and products that help stakeholders make data driven decisions.
  • Using a wide range of methods (statistical, regression, clustering, machine learning) to evaluate customer behavior.
  • Supporting stakeholders with KPI definition, performance measurement, and deeper analytical insights that drive strategy and day-to-day decision-making.
  • Proactively uncovering opportunities to improve processes, and building systems to enable scalable analysis, reporting, and data quality.
