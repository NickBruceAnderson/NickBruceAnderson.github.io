About

I have a rubik's complex.
That is, once I see the puzzle, I can't put it down until it's solved...

In my personal life, my hobbies helped scratched the itch.
In being a competition gamer, the feeling of crushing a crazy guitar lick, perfecting a YouTube edit for my educational content channel, or the warm-fuzzy from debugging faulty code.

But professionally, I needed change.
Though I found success in my beginnings on BA & PMO teams, the macro-oriented responsibilities under-utilized my technical skills and passion for getting hands-on with my work. I took command, made a career shift to data science, and found what was missing.

Data science. Data science has puzzles galore.
During my time at a Fortune 100, I made opportunites to work on data analysis, using SQL and Python to help solve customer pain points in a sea of bigdata consumer features.
Recently I've gained proficiency in more advanced SQL querying and Python machine learning techniques through scholastic work (udemy bootcamps) and pet projects, which my portfolio below details.

After taking time off to seriously consider my career options, I've found the answer.

I want to grow a career in data science.

WHAT I DO

Data Wrangling, Cleaning, Exploration

I work data science efforts on a project basis, and only after properly aligning on a business problem, I begin the data cleaning and exploration steps that are key to garnering hidden insights, predictions, and project success.

Here's how:

  • Querying Softwares:
    SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL, NoSQL
  • Data Navigation fundamentals:
    SELECT, FROM, WHERE, ORDER BY, TOP
  • Aggregate functions and viewing:
    SUM, COUNT, GROUP BY, HAVING
  • Window functions to pass data:
    PARTITION BY
  • Joining datasets:
    Proper primary-foreign key relationships, INNER JOIN, (and sometimes) OUTER/LEFT/RIGHT JOIN
  • Functions in Cleaning:
    CASE-WHEN-ELSE, CAST, CONVERT, UPDATE, ALTER, DROP
  • Techniques for Cleaning:
    field standardization, NULL imputation and/or dropping, string parsing, dupe-removal, clean feature selection
  • Coding Skills
    python: numpy, pandas, matplotlib, snsplot and R: dplyr, ggplot
  • Advanced Querying via:
    common table expressions (CTEs), nested queries, temp tables
  • Data Cleaning Mantra: Garbage-in = Garbage-out

Client Interfacing, Visualizations

After preparing clean data and deriving insights, I work towards reporting key findings in a clean, understandable way. I enjoy the details, but know the importance of translating dense analytics into actionable, human-readable outcomes.

My work here includes:

  • Formal Tableau visualizations, and PowerPoint presentations for key video/in-person conferences.
  • Zoom, WebEx, or other screenshare software for ad-hoc presentation or collaborative work
  • Command over mathematical and statistical concepts (calculus, linear algebra, hypothesis testing) and methods (regression, clustering, machine learning) to evaluate customer behavior.
  • Creation of tools and services that help stakeholders make data driven decisions.
  • Supporting business with KPI definition, performance measurement, and deeper analytical insights that drive strategy and day-to-day decision-making.
  • Proactively uncovering opportunities for process improvement, and building systems to enable scalable analysis, reporting, and data quality.
